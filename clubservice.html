What is Club Service?

Club Service Avenue focuses on the internal development, unity and effective functioning of the Rotaract Club. It ensures that all members feel connected, motivated and inspired to contribute to the club’s vision.It is the avenue that strengthens fellowship among members and promotes effective club operations through meetings, events and team-building.

Key Activities

Team Bonding Events

Organizing fun and interactive activities that strengthen friendships and teamwork among club members.

Club Meetings

Coordinating productive meetings and maintaining strong internal communication.

Fellowship Nights

Creating memorable experiences through fellowship nights, game days, and cultural events.
