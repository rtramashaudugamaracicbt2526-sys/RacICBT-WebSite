Our Club History

A Journey of Leadership, Fellowship & Service

2018-2019

The Rotaract Club of ICBT was officially established on July 1st, 2018, under the Rotary Club of Cinnamon Gardens. Rtr. Shameen Samarawickrema was installed as the charter president, marking the beginning of an exciting journey.

2019-2020

The club achieved notable milestones, receiving recognition as one of the best-emerging clubs in Rotaract District 3220. Rtr. Methmi Pinnagoda won the Golden Award for Best Secretary and served as Club Service Coordinator.

2020-2021

Under the leadership of Rtr. Basharath Isamath, the club overcame the pandemic by executing virtual projects and hosting PETS & SETS 2020, winning Silver and Bronze awards for excellence.

2021-2022

Led by Rtr. Tashya Wickramarachchi, the first female president, the club championed diversity and inclusion, earning district recognition and a Bronze distinction for the Rotaract District Citation.

2022-2023

Rtr. Aqeelah Basheer led a transformative year with impactful community service and youth development projects that reinforced the club’s strong foundation.

2023-2024

Under President Rtr. Umar Shafeek, guided by the theme "Transforming Lives, Uniting Hearts," the club executed 36 projects and won multiple awards, including the UGRAM recognition for “Korean Fusion Festival.”
